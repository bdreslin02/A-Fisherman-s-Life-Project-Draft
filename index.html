Fishing Charters

Captained by some of Tampa Bay's finest, you'll make some of your best memories on our fishing charters. From as short as four hours to as long as a whole day, you can catch your share and eat it, too!

Fishing Gear

We are your one-stop-shop for fishing gear and accessories. If you forgot something, or don't have your own stuff, don't sweat it. We'll hook you up with our premium, hand-made hardware, including stainless steel rods and reels, and durable lines, hooks, and lures.

Boat Rentals

It's a-boat time you take a sea-nic drive around Tampa Bay in one of our four boats. Equipped with state of the art technology available to you for as long as you want (up to one day!), the possibilities are endless. Must have a boating license.

LAFC Fishing Lessons

Our award-winning 'Love at First Catch' fishing lessons are a bucket list item that will elevate your skills to the next level! Take the oppor-tuna-ty to have fun while learning from our world-class instructors. All lessons open to all ages. Now available for birthdays and special events!
